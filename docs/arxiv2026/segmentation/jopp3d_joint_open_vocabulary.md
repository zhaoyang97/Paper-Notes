## 背景与动机
Semantic segmentation across visual modalities such as 3D point clouds and panoramic images remains a challenging task, primarily due to the scarcity of annotated data and the limited adaptability of fixed-label models.. In this paper, we present JOPP-3D, an open-vocabulary semantic segmentation framework that jointly leverages panoramic and point cloud data to enable language-driven scene understanding.
